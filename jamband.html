Wing it Wednesday

We wing it... mostly on Wednesdays...

If you haven't realised, our band is called Wing it Wednesday. We are a jam band from Pioneer Hall, in which I have been playing the keys for since 2020. Typically, hall jam bands are involved in performances within hall, or even within the wider NTU. We have been performing at the Nanyang Arts Festival (NAF) for the past 2 years, a cultural festival held annually in NTU. Because of Covid-19, our performances have been limited a lot to recordings, which in itself is also quite an interesting experience. However, as measures relax, we recently just had our first live performance in hall! You can check out our recordings and performances in the other section!
